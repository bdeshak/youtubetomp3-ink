Amazon Wedding Registry is a service offered by Amazon that allows engaged couples to create a wishlist of items they'd like to receive as gifts for their wedding. It's a convenient way for couples to let their guests know what they need or want, and for guests to easily purchase and send gifts directly to the couple.

Here's how Amazon Wedding Registry typically works

1. **Creating the Registry**: The engaged couple creates a wedding registry on Amazon. They can add a variety of items to the registry, such as household items, appliances, electronics, furniture, and more.

2. **Sharing the Registry**: The couple shares the registry link with their wedding guests, either through invitations, wedding websites, or social media. This allows guests to view the registry and select items to purchase.

3. **Guest Purchases**: Guests can browse the registry, choose items they want to give as gifts, and purchase them directly from Amazon. They can then choose to have the items shipped to the couple's preferred address.

4. **Gift Tracking**: Amazon provides tools for the couple to track the gifts that have been purchased from their registry. This helps them keep track of who bought which gift and whether the item has been fulfilled.

5. **Completion Discount**: Amazon often offers a completion discount, which allows the couple to purchase any remaining items on their registry at a discounted price after the wedding.

6. **Shipping and Returns**: Amazon provides shipping options, including gift wrapping, and offers a return and exchange policy for items purchased from the registry.

Amazon Wedding Registry provides a user-friendly interface, making it easy for both couples and guests to use the service. It's a convenient way to manage and organize wedding gifts, ensuring that the couple receives items they truly want and need.
